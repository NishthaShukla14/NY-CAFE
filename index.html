Our Story

At Niyash, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink—it's an experience. Founded with a passion for bringing people together, our cozy haven offers the perfect blend of modern comfort and artisanal craftsmanship.

We source only the finest quality ingredients, working closely with local roasters and bakers to ensure every cup and every bite brings joy to your day. Whether you're here to work, relax, or catch up with friends, Niyash is your home away from home.
